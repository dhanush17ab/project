Signature Dish: Royal Seafood Platter

This exquisite dish features a selection of the finest seafood, including lobster, prawns, oysters, and scallops, served with a variety of sauces and garnishes.

Ingredients

The Royal Seafood Platter includes lobster, king prawns, freshly shucked oysters, scallops, and a variety of herbs and spices for a burst of flavor.

Cooking Method

Each item in the platter is carefully grilled, steamed, or roasted to perfection, ensuring that the natural flavors of the seafood shine through.

Side Dishes

To complement the main platter, we offer a selection of side dishes, including garlic butter asparagus, roasted vegetables, and a side of crispy fries.

Serving Suggestion

We recommend pairing the Royal Seafood Platter with a chilled glass of Chardonnay or a refreshing white wine for the ultimate dining experience.
