DBIT College Departments

Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department offers a variety of programs focused on software engineering, data science, artificial intelligence, and more.

Key areas of study include:

  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science
  • Cybersecurity
  • Artificial Intelligence

Electrical Engineering Department

The Electrical Engineering Department prepares students for a career in electrical systems, circuits, and power generation.

Key areas of study include:

  • Power Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Embedded Systems
  • Signal Processing

Mechanical Engineering Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department is known for its research in thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and materials science.

Key areas of study include:

  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Material Science
  • Robotics

Biology Department

The Biology Department offers a wide range of courses in cellular biology, genetics, and environmental science.

Key areas of study include:

  • Cellular Biology
  • Genetics
  • Ecology
  • Microbiology
